# PRODUCT.md — Nextudy

register: brand

## Product purpose
Premium lead-generation landing page for **Nextudy**, a mentor-led BIM / AEC upskilling
startup. The page exists to build trust and convert visitors to "Book a free career call."
It is the product's first impression, so the design IS the product (brand register).

## Users
- Students and fresh graduates (20-27): civil / mechanical / architecture grads, diploma holders, curious about BIM careers.
- Working professionals (27-30): CAD draftsmen, site engineers, junior architects / BIM modelers looking to level up or switch into BIM roles.
They are ambitious, slightly anxious about employability, and skeptical of cheap "institute" marketing.

## Brand
Mentor-led, practical, industry-relevant. Confident without hype. The feeling to leave:
"These people know where the industry is going, and they prepare you for it." Tagline: "Skill up now."

## Tone
Concrete, grounded, plain-spoken. Name the real pain (your degree did not teach Revit) and
the real fix (mentors who ship projects, a portfolio that gets interviews). Never breathless.

## Anti-references (do NOT look like)
- Generic tuition-center / college / government-training websites.
- Template SaaS with hero-metric blocks, three identical feature cards, gradient text.
- Stock photos of smiling students in classrooms.
- Fake statistics, fake company logos, placement / salary guarantees.

## Strategic principles
- Trust over decoration, but the craft must signal "serious startup, premium delivery."
- Orange (#f9a11d) is the dominant accent in every register.
- Motion is disciplined and cinematic, never gimmicky.
- Every claim is honest; capability, not promises.
